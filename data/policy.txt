HOME INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Policy Holder: Customer
Policy Type: Home Insurance

----------------------------
COVERAGE
----------------------------
1. Fire Damage:
   Loss or damage caused by fire is covered.

2. Water Damage:
   Damage caused by burst pipes, leaking plumbing, or internal water systems is covered.

3. Theft:
   Theft of contents from within the insured premises is covered.

4. Storm Damage:
   Damage caused by storms is covered only if wind speed exceeds the defined threshold.

----------------------------
EXCLUSIONS
----------------------------
1. Flood Damage:
   Damage caused by external flooding, heavy rainfall, or water ingress from outside is not covered.

2. Negligence:
   Damage caused due to lack of maintenance or negligence is not covered.

3. Intentional Damage:
   Any intentional or fraudulent damage is excluded.

----------------------------
LIMITS
----------------------------
1. Jewellery:
   Maximum coverage ₹50,000 per item unless endorsement is purchased.

2. Electronics:
   Maximum coverage ₹1,00,000.

3. Total Claim Limit:
   Maximum claim payout ₹5,00,000 per incident.

----------------------------
DEDUCTIBLE
----------------------------
A deductible of ₹5,000 applies to each claim.

----------------------------
ENDORSEMENTS
----------------------------
If additional coverage is purchased:
- Jewellery limit can be extended up to ₹2,00,000.
- Electronics coverage can be increased.

If no endorsement is mentioned, default limits apply.
