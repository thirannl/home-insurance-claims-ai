TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. STORM CONDITIONS:
   Storm damage is only covered if wind speed exceeds Force 8 on the Beaufort scale.

2. OCCUPANCY CONDITION:
   The house must not be left unoccupied for more than 30 consecutive days.

3. VALID POLICY PERIOD:
   The incident must occur within the active policy period.

4. FRAUD CHECK:
   Any fraudulent claims will be rejected.

5. PARTIAL COVERAGE:
   If claim amount exceeds limits, only the allowed amount will be paid.

6. HUMAN REVIEW:
   If information is missing or unclear, the claim must be referred for human review.
